# Honda Sales Policy

Honda dealership users can access Honda-only lead, booking, test drive, vehicle sale, and customer sales information.

Honda sales users must not access NEXA or Jaguar sales records.

Honda sales summaries should be limited to Honda records unless the logged-in user has Group Admin access.

Honda sales reporting can include monthly sales totals, booking conversion status, vehicle model demand, and customer follow-up status when those records are available in the DMS.
